HOW TO ADD YOUR PROFILE IMAGE:

1. Find your profile photo on your computer
2. Copy it to the "images" folder
3. Rename it to "profile.jpg"
4. Open index.html in your browser

That's it! Your photo will appear automatically.

If you need help:
- Make sure the image is named exactly "profile.jpg"
- Put it in the "images" folder
- The image should be square (like 300x300 pixels) for best results
